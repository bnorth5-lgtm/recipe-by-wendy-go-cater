## The Heritage Collection

This collection is our origin story—told in plates and pours.

Rooted in the **Cronkhite legacy** and shaped by a **Stonington connection**, The Heritage Collection draws from classic New England sensibilities elevated with modern technique. Think coastal brightness, garden depth, and a grounded elegance that never tries too hard.

**A Heritage Collection experience often includes**
- **Welcome bites** that set the tone (coastal, crisp, and intentional)
- **Seasonal first course** (garden-forward with refined restraint)
- **Signature entrée** (a heritage-inspired centerpiece with modern balance)
- **Side compositions** designed for texture, color, and flow
- **Final sweet** that lands softly—clean, luminous, unforgettable

**Designed for**
- Weddings with a sense of place
- Rehearsal dinners and family weekends
- Intimate celebrations where story matters as much as service

A five-course, curated experience built for grand entrances and long applause.

The Black-Tie Gala is a **5-course progression** created to move like a narrative: anticipation, brightness, depth, warmth, finale. It’s paced for speeches, performances, and dancing—each course arriving with timing that feels effortless.

**The 5-course cadence (curated for your event)**

- **Course III: Velvet & Fire** — a warm, layered course with elegant richness

**Perfect for**

## The Signature Loaf

Blueberry-cranberry bread, told the way it should be: from story to table.

The Signature Loaf begins as a memory—**blueberries and cranberries** in a tender crumb, the kind of loaf that feels like an heirloom even the first time you taste it. We bake it with a balance of brightness and depth: fruit-forward without being sweet, structured without being heavy.

**Story-to-Table**
- **The story**: a loaf that belongs at the center of a family gathering—warm, generous, quietly luxurious.
- **The table**: served sliced and styled for the moment—paired with cultured butter, seasonal preserves, or as a late-night wedding reprise.

**How it’s featured**
- As a welcome offering during bridal or gala prep
- Alongside a curated course as a heritage accent
- As a farewell gift—boxed with a note that feels personal
